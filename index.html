About Me

My name is Sharlae Eliza Brown. I was born on January 22nd, 2001 at Providence Little Company of Mary Medical Center In Torrance California. I currently reside in Virgina due to having joined the United States Air Force when I was 18. I am an Aerospace Propulsion Journeyman full time, and a computer science major with an associates degree in Aviation Maintenance Technology.

When I am not working or pursuing academic studies, I like to engage in a few of my hobbies. Some of these include home improvement,cooking or baking, and working on my car. I bought my first home one year ago. Since then, I have completely remodeled my bathroom, updated my appliances, created a home gym, and am currently redecorating my master bedroom. Now that I have given my house a home feel, I like to prepare some of my favorite dishes as a form of relaxation. The number one meal I like to prepare is chicken tacos for myself and others. Outside of home, every now and then I add upgrades to my vehicle such as new shift boots, dash cam mirrors, and a back up camera; I have been in four car accidents making this a necessity. These are very small changes, but have made my vehicle feel more like my own.
